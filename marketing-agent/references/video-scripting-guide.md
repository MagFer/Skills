# Video Scripting Guide for App Promos

## Video Types & Length

| Type | Length | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| App Store Preview | 15-30s | Show core feature loop, auto-plays in store |
| Social Promo | 30-60s | Hook + demo + CTA for Twitter/Instagram/TikTok |
| Feature Spotlight | 30-45s | Deep dive on one feature |
| Launch Announcement | 45-90s | Full overview for major releases |

## Script Structure

### 1. Hook (0-3 seconds)
Start with a question, bold statement, or relatable pain point.

**Examples:**
- "Do you actually know your net worth?"
- "Your finances are everywhere. What if they weren't?"
- "Stocks, crypto, real estate — one app, one number."

### 2. Problem (3-8 seconds)
Show the frustration briefly — scattered apps, manual spreadsheets, no clear picture.

### 3. Solution (8-20 seconds)
Introduce Impulse. Show the app in action:
- Adding an asset (quick tap flow)
- Portfolio view updating in real time
- Multi-currency support
- Beautiful charts/graphs

### 4. Key Differentiators (20-35 seconds)
Pick 2-3 from:
- Track stocks, crypto, ETFs, real estate, and loans in one place
- Real-time price updates
- Privacy-first — your data stays on your device
- Multi-currency with automatic conversion
- Manual entries for anything (cars, watches, art)
- Clean, native iOS design

### 5. CTA (last 3-5 seconds)
- "Download Impulse — free on the App Store"
- "Try Impulse today" + App Store badge
- End on the portfolio screen showing a satisfying total

## Shot List Template

```
Shot #  | Duration | Visual                        | Audio/Text Overlay
--------|----------|-------------------------------|-------------------
1       | 3s       | Close-up: phone, app opening  | "Know your worth"
2       | 5s       | Portfolio view, numbers update | "Everything in one place"
3       | 4s       | Adding a stock via search      | "Add in seconds"
4       | 4s       | Manual entry (property)        | "Track anything"
5       | 3s       | Portfolio total with graph     | App Store badge + CTA
```

## Recording Tips

- Use iPhone screen recording (Settings > Control Center > Screen Recording)
- Record at native resolution — don't crop or resize
- Clean up notifications and status bar before recording
- Use sample data that looks realistic but isn't real financial info
- Slow down interactions slightly so viewers can follow
- Record in both light and dark mode — pick the one that fits the platform

## Audio Guidelines

- Background music: upbeat, modern, royalty-free (check licensing)
- No voiceover needed for App Store previews (most watch muted)
- Social videos benefit from voiceover or captions
- Always add captions — most social video is watched without sound

## Platform-Specific Notes

### App Store Preview
- Max 3 previews per locale
- Must be actual app footage (no added graphics outside the app)
- Auto-plays muted in the store — visual storytelling is key
- Resolution must match device (iPhone 17 Pro: 1290x2796)

### Instagram Reels / TikTok
- Vertical (9:16)
- Hook must land in first 1-2 seconds
- Trending audio can boost reach
- Add text overlays for key points

### Twitter/X
- Horizontal (16:9) or square (1:1)
- Keep under 2:20 (Twitter limit)
- First frame matters — it's the thumbnail
